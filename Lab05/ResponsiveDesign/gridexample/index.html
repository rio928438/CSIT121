Located in the Heart of the Desert

At Desert Bed & Breakfast you’ll be close to art galleries, shops, restaurants, hiking trails, and tours. Ride the free trolley to shops and galleries.

Luxurious Rooms

Stay in a well-appointed room at Desert B&B with your own fireplace, king-size bed, balcony overlooking the red rocks, private bath with shower, and whirlpool tub for two. Each vista is unique and sure to be a high point of your vacation experience.

Hearty Breakfast

Each day begins with a hearty breakfast that includes your choice of belgian waffles, pumpkin french toast, or quiche. Fresh fruit, yogurt, juice, and coffee are always available.

About Your Hosts

Your hosts are long-time Desert residents who love to share their knowledge of the Desert area with their guests.

Guided Hikes

We offer a guided hike each week for guests. Explore the areas along our famous trails.
