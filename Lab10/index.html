Web of Things

What is it?

The web of things is a fairy new concept. It is like a type of internet for smart devices. This can include but isn't limited to; Fridges, Microwaves, Dishwashers, Washer and Dryer, Lights, Smart Tvs. All these devices would be set to interact on a Hub. a Hub would be either an app on your phone or a system or program on a computer.

Think about the Jetsons if youre old like me or like any episode of Rick and Morty! The Web of Things is modern way for devices to communicate with each other to provide a futuristic smart home experience. For example, say you come home from work, what if as soon as you walk in the door, the lights turn on, the stove turns on, some decaff coffee is made and so on. There are some many applications, and to flip it around think of the Morning. You wake up, sunlight colored light starts dim and within 25 minutes is fully bright, your coffee starts your toaster starts as well like back in the future, Doc Brown Styles!
